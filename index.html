What is this game?

League of Legends is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) genre video game developed by Riot Games. 
It was designed to operate on the Microsoft Windows operating system. 
The game was first announced on October 7, 2008 and released a year later on October 27, 2009.
The game was in beta from April 10, 2009 to October 26th, 2009.
